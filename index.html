DevIgnition

November 10, 2023

DevIgnition is a fun and informal community event run for and by the DC software development community by the DC JUG. Come discover how the best minds use the latest technologies to build solutions. Network with other DC area software developers, and discuss the newest emerging trends.

DevIgnition features a full day of presentations on a wide variety of technical topics from noted professional developers. The presentations trend toward emerging technologies.
