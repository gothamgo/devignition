Video Adapters

Please bring your HDMI video adapter (your laptop to HDMI). The venue uses HDMI. They have new projectors. I have an adapter from USB-C to HDMI, but adapters sometimes get lost and we are left looking for one at the last minute. Please bring yours.

Try the Projection

Please try your presentation as soon as you can after you get there, either in the morning or a break. Make sure you have the mirroring, resolution, etc. set up the way you want before your talk. It is much better to have those issues solved before your talk begins! There will be power at the podium. The venue will be open to us starting at 7 AM. We will definately be there by 8 AM.

Wireless Internet Access

Wireless will be available, but as always it might not work! Please do not depend upon it. Have a version of your presentation on your laptop, if possible. You will need to get a wireless account to use while there. They have instructions posted in the venue on how to do that. It is very easy.

The projection is best at 16:9 or 4:3 aspect ratio, but 16:9 will look better.

Please avoid small text on your slides.

Location, Getting There, Parking, etc.

We are at GMU's Arlington VA Van Metre Hall, rooms 125 and 126 (the large ballroom, on the left side of the building as seen from the street). Parking is possible under the building. You have to go around the back of the building into the alley to get in there, though.

Also, the Virginia Square and Clarendon metro stops are just a couple of blocks from GMU, a 5 minute walk away (Orange and Silver DC Metro lines).
